Plastic2048

HTML, CSS, JS

April 2020

As a final project for an experience and interaction course, students were asked to work on their own to produce a screen-based experience, preferably with interactivity.

The main motivation for this project is the sad fact that our oceans and marine life are choking on plastic. To draw the connection between our everyday action and their impacts on the world, I decided to use an interactive game as a medium. Plastic2048 is inspired by the game 2048, which was created by Gabriele Cirulli.

Monineath Heng | Experience and Interaction
