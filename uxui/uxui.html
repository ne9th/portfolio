As a semester-long project for an interaction design course, students were asked to design a web-based solution to a unique problem that they face in their life.

Since I have 2 siblings who are allergic to certain ingredients in food/medication, I decided to design a website that would help them with their allergies.

To arrive at the final prototype, I conducted interviews, benchmarked against existing service, created personas, defined user's roles and goals, and designed branding.

lost and found

Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop

April 2021

As a final project for an interaction design course, students were asked to conduct research and come up with an application that solves a problem for a certain population.

To challenge myself, I decided to choose the elderly population as my target audience. Since we're all affected by COVID-19, the only person I can visit at the time was my grandma. So I went and talked to her and saw that she was having problems with misplacing things and forgetting where things are.

So with my grandma in mind, Lost & Found was born.
