Typography Poster

InDesign, Illustrator

July 2020

As a project for a typography class, students were asked to define and show example of 21 type terms, 28 letter form parts, 1 perfectly set paragraph and 1 paragraph with 5 common type inconsistences.

I came across a beautiful picture of a flower shop while I was scrolling on Pinterest one day. Since flowers seemed to have endless possibilities to play with, I decided to go with this theme.

Landmark Poster

InDesign

Feb 2021

As a project for a design history course, each student was assigned a landmark in the history of design. We then had to write an essay about our subject's historical importance and design a 15x30in poster about it.

My subject was the sign program by Carbone Smolan Agency at Musée du Louvre in France.

Logotypes & Typefaces Book

InDesign, Illustrator

May 2020

As a project for a typography course, students were asked to create a book that explores 24 typefaces and 24 corresponding logotypes.

I decided to go with Pantone colors as the theme for this project because I've always loved working with colors. And the minimal design of Pantone chips is also something that I've wanted to work with.

Service Day Project

Photoshop

May 2019

While I was working as a graphic designer at Suffolk University, I had this awesome opportunity to design the entire project, from poster to t-shirt, for the 22nd Annual Service Day.

P.S. I did this entire project in Photoshop because at the time, I had no experience with any advanced design software. And since Photoshop was a name I was most familiar with, I decided to go with it. :)
